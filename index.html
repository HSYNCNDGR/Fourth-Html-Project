Chocolate Cookies Recipe

Ingredients of Chocolate Cookies-5 Servings


  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 11/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • salt as required

    Step 1 Whisk the butter, egg and sugar

  1. Take a large bowl and whisk butter with sugar for a few seconds using an electric beater, till it gets light and fluffy. Beat an egg in a different bowl till it becomes yellow and creamy.

    2. Step 2 Add vanilla essence to the mix

  2. Then add this creamy egg mixture to the butter-sugar bowl and mix well using a spoon. Also, add vanilla extract and mix the ingredients well. Take another bowl and add all purpose flour in it.

    3. Step 3 Make a thick batter

  3. Now, add cocoa powder and baking soda in the bowl of flour and mix salt in it. Once the flour mixture is done, add butter-egg mixture in the flour bowl and whisk well. Afterwards, add chocolate chips in this mixture and stir well.

    4. Step 4 Bake the cookies

  4. Now, preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Take a baking sheet and put a spoonful of creamed mixture onto the baking sheet. Make sure you leave some space in between these spoonfuls of the batter.

    5. Step 5 Cook for 10 minutes

  5. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake them for about 10 minutes. Once they are done, take them out and let them cool at room temperature.

    6. Step 6 Ready to be served

  6. The soft and fluffy chocolate cookies are ready to relish! Do try this tempting cookie recipe and don't forget to leave your comments in the section below.
